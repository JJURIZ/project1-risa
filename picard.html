Picard, Vash, and the Tox Uthat Adventure

The most famous visitor to Risa was Starfleet Captain Jean-Luc Picard. While on shore leave in 2366, Picard was unwittingly roped into an archaeological heist involving con-woman Vash, cheated Ferengi Sovak, and two time traveling Vorgon. The object of all this attention? The Tox Uthat.

It is claimed the Tox Uthat was hidden on Risa by its inventor, Kal Dano. According to legend, Dano traveled back to the 22nd century and hid the Uthat on Risa to keep nefarious Vorgons from obtaining the device.

The Tox Uthatâ€™s value derives from its ability to halt nuclear fission in a star. The potential destructive power made Risa, a peaceful planet, the seemingly ideal hiding place.

The vacationing Captain Picard worked with Vash to keep the Tox Uthat out of Ferengi and Vorgon hands. Realizing the potential for chaos, Picard ordered his ship The USS Enterprise to destroy the Uthat.
