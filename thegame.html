Keeping Your Wits on Risa

The vast majority of Risan visitors are here for one thing - a good time! But occasionally a “bad actor” enters the scene and causes trouble for one of our visitors.

Commander William T. Riker of the USS Enterprise (yes, that Enterprise!) made one of his many trips to Risa when he encountered Etana Jol, a Ktarian woman.

Commander Riker was introduced to an augmented reality game by the Ktarian. Instead of pursuing more play from Etana, his attention turned exclusively to the game headset.

Unbeknownst to Riker, the game was actually a mind control device Etana Jol created and Riker replicated on the Enterprise. Soon everyone on the ship was under Etana Jol’s control. A receptive crew member was able to discover and defuse the plot just in time to avert disaster.

We share this story with you in hopes that you will not completely abandon your wits when staying with us.
