Risa - An Overview

Before becoming the go-to destination in the Federation, Risa was a desolate and inhospitable planet. With the help of weather control systems Risa was transformed into paradise. Tourism became (and remains) the number one source of activity for the planet’s inhabitants.

Risa became synonymous with pleasure. Whether to relax and swim in the crystal clear waters or partake in the jamaharon rite, creatures from across the quadrants flock to Risa.

Climate

Risa maintains a climate described as "the equivalent of a balmy eighty-three degrees Fahrenheit", and that "a warm breeze prevents the heat from becoming oppressive".

Emphasis on Pleasure

Risa is dedicated to pleasure. This is why no weapons are permitted on Risa. Guests on Risa feel secure knowing their safety is a priority.

Though the origin of Jamaharon is unknown, it has become well known far and wide. Those seeking to participate in the rite of Jamaharon must obtain a Horga’hn. Once the Horga’hn is displayed Risan’s will approach the willing participant and ask about their intentions.

Things to Know about Risa

  • Located in the Alpha Quadrant
  • The closest starbase to Risa is Federation Starbase 12
  • Two moons orbin Risa

  • Ninety lightyears from the Sol system.
